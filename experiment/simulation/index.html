Instructions
  • This experiment is designed to teach how to construct lattices.
  • The left side of the UI shows the current atom being used to construct the lattice and the target lattice which the simlulation expects the students to build.
  • The section below provides various utilities like 'Add atom'(add atoms at the position of the mouse click) , 'Add atom at a Coordintate' ( to add atoms at the specified coordintates), 'select Atom'( enable / disable selecting atom mode).
  • After adding the atoms and selecting them, use 'select region'( to create a convex hull) toe visualize the convex hull which encapsulates the selected atoms.
  • After selecting the atoms which the student thinks form the lattice of interest. Clicking 'Check packing' will validate if the lattice contructed is valid or not. The results will be dispalyed on the obesrvation section on the right.
